# Simulated Phishing Email Template

## Subject
Security Alert: Password Expiration Notice

## Sender Name
IT Support Team

## Email Body
Hello,

Your account password is scheduled to expire today. To avoid disruption in access, please review your account status and complete the password update process through the internal portal.

Thank you,  
IT Support Team

## Psychological Triggers Used
- Urgency
- Authority
- Fear of account disruption

## Purpose
This email is designed to simulate a common phishing scenario in a controlled security awareness exercise.
