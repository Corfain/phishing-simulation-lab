# Campaign Metrics

## Sample Results
- Emails Delivered: 10
- Emails Opened: 7
- Links Clicked: 4
- Credentials Submitted: 2

## Calculated Rates
- Open Rate: 70%
- Click Rate: 40%
- Credential Submission Rate: 20%

## Analysis
The results indicate that a significant portion of users interacted with the phishing content. The credential submission rate shows that some users were unable to identify the simulated phishing indicators.

## Risk Interpretation
- High open rate suggests the email subject was convincing
- Click activity indicates insufficient link inspection habits
- Credential submission suggests a need for stronger awareness training and technical controls
